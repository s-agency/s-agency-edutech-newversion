3Skill 2-Month Internship – FAQs

All you need to know about our internship, projects, benefits & certification.

Our 2-Month Summer Internship is divided into two phases — Month 1 is live classes with expert mentors, and Month 2 is a hands-on project phase where you build real industry projects. You also get a certificate and a chance to visit IIT Kharagpur campus.

Any student from any college or university can join — 1st year to final year. Whether you're from CSE, IT, or any other branch, as long as you're eager to learn and build, you're welcome!

We currently offer 4 domains: AI & Machine Learning, Web Development, Data Analytics, and Frontend Development. Each domain has dedicated mentors, live sessions, and project work.

Month 1: Live online classes, doubt sessions, assignments and learning resources. Month 2: Project building phase where you work on a real industry problem independently with mentor guidance.

Top performers are considered for paid internship roles at 3Skill and our partner companies. We actively help students grow from free interns to paid interns based on their project performance.

You only pay ₹499 as a slot booking fee to reserve your seat in the internship batch. The amount is 100% refundable with simple terms & conditions. There are no hidden charges — the fee is just to confirm serious applicants and secure your slot.

Yes! Along with the internship training, you will receive learning materials, project resources, and 1000+ DSA practice questions and interview preparation questions completely free of cost. These resources will help you improve problem-solving skills and prepare for technical interviews.

Top performers from each batch are selected for an exclusive IIT Kharagpur campus visit. This is based on your project quality, consistency, and overall performance during the internship.

Yes! Every student who completes the 2-month program receives an official 3Skill Internship Completion Certificate — recognized by companies and useful for your resume and LinkedIn profile.

Our entire 2-month internship program is conducted through live classes. There are no pre-recorded videos — every session is taken live by mentors where you can ask questions and clear doubts in real time. You will also receive the recordings of the live sessions so you can revise the concepts anytime.

Simply click the "Book My Slot – ₹499" button, choose your domain, and complete the slot booking. Our team will reach out to you with onboarding details before the batch starts.
